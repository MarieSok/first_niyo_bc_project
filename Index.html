A bit about me

Hi,

My name is Perpetua.

I am an interdisciplinary artist and creative technologist. As theatre maker, I have been commissioned by the Royal Court and worked with the London cast of Hamilton. In the world of film, I have had the pleasure of working at an Emmy-award nominated production company. As a dancer, I am currently deepening my artistic practice at Sadler's Wells and Siobhan Davies Studios. Previous to entering the worl of creative technology, I worked in advertising with brands like Google, adidas and Penguin Roundhouse. I am excited to be starting my creative technology project 'Dancing Differently', a disruptive project which aims to dismantle the inherent ableism in dance by leveraging immersive technology to make the invisibility of neurodiversity, visible.
